Claridge’s, London

The famed art deco building dates back to 1898 and its grand foyer and sweeping staircase make a memorable first impression. The 203 guestrooms and suites are the largest in London, individually designed in shades of white, gold, pastel blue and silver. The afternoon tea in the lobby is legendary and the health and fitness centre has personal trainers and views across Hyde Park.

Our members receive complimentary breakfast, early check-in/late check-out*, complimentary room upgrade*, £60 food and beverage credit and a small welcome gift.

The Upper House, Hong Kong

Located in central Hong Kong near Pacific Place, the city’s main shopping district, the property is modern and full of intriguing spaces, incorporating natural materials with playful lighting created by leading designer Andre Fu. The 117 rooms are all light and spacious with spectacular views across the city through the substantial windows, and all feature free-standing baths.

Our members receive complimentary breakfast, early check-in/late check-out*, complimentary room upgrade* and US$100 hotel credit.

Book a stay at The Upper House, Hong Kong

One&Only The Palm, Dubai

Set in a prime position on Dubai’s iconic man-made palm-shaped island, this beautiful hotel is a great alternative to the city’s larger, more flamboyant options. Blending Moorish and Andalusian architecture, guestrooms are divided between the main manor house and beachfront villas with gardens and balconies. There’s a private stretch of beach dotted with huge day beds, three excellent restaurants and a state-of-the-art spa.

Our members receive complimentary breakfast, early check-in/late check-out*, complimentary room upgrade*, dinner for two people per stay (excluding alcohol), a welcome cocktail.
